    • About Us

    Stanford capital is leading outsourcing firm based in Mumbai and UAE. We are solution providers in the areas of Finance and Accounts, compliance expertise, Information Technology, Human Resources and Business Process Outsourcing. We strive to provide innovative and practical solutions that can be easily implemented. As a solution provider, we empathize with our clients and prioritize understanding their business and tackling their challenges as if they were our own.

    Our Process improves quality, reduces cost and time of business processes requirements.

    We consider ourselves partners to our clients, rather than mere service providers. This mindset enables us to work in their best interests, leaving no stone unturned to ensure that even the most intricate assignments are managed seamlessly.

    With years of experience, knowledge and integrity we become reliable extensions for our clients so they can focus on their core competencies. We have a team of 50+ professionals which includes Chartered Accountants, Cost Accountants, Company Secretaries, Lawyers, Financial Advisors and Management Consultants having cumulative experience of more than 30+ years with consulting firms and corporates.
